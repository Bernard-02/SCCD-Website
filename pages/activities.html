ACTIVITIES

From hands-on workshops and degree shows to summer camps and student-led initiatives, SCCD's activities enrich creative learning and foster a vibrant community of designers.

從工作營、畢業展到暑期體驗營與學生自主活動，學系透過多元的課外活動豐富學生的創意學習歷程，凝聚充滿活力的設計社群。

SCCD hosts and participates in various exhibitions throughout the year, showcasing student works and creative research across animation, interactive media, and visual design.

學系定期舉辦與參與各類展演活動，展示學生在動畫、互動媒體與視覺設計等領域的創作成果與研究實踐。

Workshops at SCCD bring together students and industry professionals for intensive hands-on learning, exploring themes across design, technology, and creative practice.

工作營匯聚學生與業界專業人士，透過密集的實作課程，深入探索設計、科技與創意實踐等多元議題。

The annual Degree Show is the culminating showcase of graduating students' final projects, presenting their creative vision and professional development to the public.

年度畢業展是應屆畢業生展示畢業製作的重要舞台，向社會大眾呈現學生的創作理念與專業成長歷程。

SCCD regularly invites distinguished designers, artists, and industry leaders to share their insights and experiences through public lectures and talks.

學系定期邀請傑出設計師、藝術家及業界領袖，透過公開講座與演講分享其專業洞見與實踐經驗。

Study visits take students to design institutions, studios, and cultural venues both locally and internationally, broadening their perspectives beyond the classroom.

參訪活動帶領學生走訪國內外設計機構、工作室與文化場所，拓展課堂之外的視野與實務見識。

SCCD students actively participate in national and international design competitions, demonstrating their creative capabilities and earning recognition on the global stage.

學系學生積極參與國內外設計競賽，展現創作實力，並在國際舞台上獲得廣泛肯定與殊榮。

Academic conferences and symposiums organized or attended by SCCD foster dialogue between design research, education, and professional practice.

學系舉辦或參與的學術研討會，促進設計研究、教育與專業實踐之間的對話與交流。

Student-initiated projects and presentations highlight the independent creative spirit of SCCD students, from self-organized events to original research and productions.

學生自主活動展現學系學生的獨立創作精神，從自辦活動到原創研究與製作，充分體現學習的主動性。

Industry-academia cooperation projects connect students with real-world design challenges, fostering collaboration between SCCD and leading companies and organizations.

產學合作計畫連結學生與真實設計議題，促進學系與各大企業及機構的合作，將學術研究落地於實務應用。
