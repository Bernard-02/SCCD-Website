ABOUT

Discover the vision, mission, and values that drive the Shih Chien University Center for Creative Design.

探索實踐大學媒體傳達設計學系的願景、使命與核心價值。

Department of Communications Design, Shih Chien University / SCCD is the first academic institution to establish the "Communications Design" department in the country. The teaching of the department takes design thinking, humanities, and aesthetics as the cornerstones, uses multidimensional platforms as the foundation, diverse media as the carrier, and integrates teaching experiments and design training such as animation, narrative video, interaction, performance, game, product, space. and environment. Supplemented by intensive international workshop and industry-academy cooperation in various fields, as well as overseas exchange opportunities. It aims to cultivate director-type creative talents who can integrate "media" as the vehicle, "design" as the method, emphasize "communications" as the core of human civilization, and adapt to the rapid changes of the era.
實踐大學媒體傳達設計學系為全國首創成立「媒體傳達設計」之學系。學系教學以設計思考、人文、美學為核心基石，運用多維度平台為基礎與多樣性媒材為載體，整合動畫、敘事影像、互動、展演、遊戲、商品、空間、環境等教學實驗與設計訓練，輔以密集舉辦之跨國工作與各領域產學合作，同時提供海外交換留學機會，旨在培育能整合「媒體」為乘載，「設計」為方法，強調「傳達」為人類文明之核心，得以適應時代快速變遷之總監型創作人才。

MDES Class

碩士班

The MDES program offers advanced research and creative practice opportunities, fostering innovation in communications design. Graduate students engage in cutting-edge projects that push the boundaries of design thinking and media expression.
碩士班提供進階研究與創作實踐機會，培養媒體傳達設計的創新能力。研究生參與前瞻性專案，推進設計思維與媒體表達的界限。
Through intensive research methodologies and collaborative workshops, MDES students develop critical perspectives on contemporary design challenges. The program emphasizes both theoretical foundations and practical applications in emerging media contexts.
透過密集的研究方法與合作工作坊，碩士班學生對當代設計挑戰發展批判性觀點。課程強調理論基礎與新興媒體情境中的實際應用。

Alumni

系友發展

SCCD Alumni serve in various fields related to digital media or entertainment and creative industries, and many of them are engaged in cultural administration, art education, etc., or continue their studies in related fields at domestic and foreign, and actively participate in various arts , multimedia and other cross-border exhibitions. In addition to exhibiting, performing, and publishing books in various personal unique creative forms, alumni of the department also cooperate with many performing individuals and groups in the field of contemporary art performance, and participate in assisting in the planning, execution, and coordinating the production of various projects.
實踐大學媒體傳達設計學系畢業系友在各行各業均有任職人員或創業者，從設計、藝術、影視娛樂、多媒體、行銷公關甚至科技公司等，以設計思考橫跨多領域均表現傑出；於國外相關領域深造學術領域或海外就職者也為數甚多，積極於各產業領域發揮其美學與設計思考之影響力。
