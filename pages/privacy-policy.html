Privacy Policy

隱私權政策

Department of Communications Design, Shih Chien University (SCCD) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website.

實踐大學媒體傳達設計學系（SCCD）致力於保護您的隱私權。本隱私權政策說明我們如何在您訪問我們的網站時收集、使用及保護您的個人資訊。

1. Information We Collect 我們收集的資訊

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring website addresses
  • Device and operating system information

當您訪問我們的網站時，我們可能會自動收集某些技術資訊，包括：

  • IP 位址和瀏覽器類型
  • 訪問的頁面和在網站上停留的時間
  • 來源網站位址
  • 設備和操作系統資訊

If you contact us through email or contact forms, we collect the information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, and message content.

如果您通過電子郵件或聯絡表單與我們聯繫，我們會收集您自願提供的資訊，例如您的姓名、電子郵件地址和訊息內容。

2. How We Use Your Information 我們如何使用您的資訊

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To respond to your inquiries and requests
  • To provide academic and administrative information
  • To analyze website usage and traffic patterns
  • To maintain website security and prevent misuse

我們將收集的資訊用於以下目的：

  • 改善我們的網站和使用者體驗
  • 回應您的查詢和請求
  • 提供學術和行政資訊
  • 分析網站使用情況和流量模式
  • 維護網站安全並防止濫用

3. Data Protection and Security 資料保護與安全

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

我們採取適當的技術和組織措施來保護您的個人資訊，防止未經授權的訪問、更改、披露或破壞。然而，沒有任何通過互聯網傳輸的方法是 100% 安全的，我們無法保證絕對的安全性。

4. Information Sharing 資訊共享

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With your explicit consent

我們不會出售、交易或出租您的個人資訊給第三方。我們僅在以下情況下共享資訊：

  • 法律或法律程序要求時
  • 保護我們的權利、財產或安全時
  • 經過您的明確同意

5. External Links 外部連結

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

我們的網站可能包含外部網站的連結。我們不對這些外部網站的隱私慣例或內容負責。我們建議您閱讀您訪問的任何第三方網站的隱私政策。

6. Your Rights 您的權利

Under Taiwan's Personal Data Protection Act, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

根據台灣個人資料保護法，您有權：

  • 要求查閱您的個人資訊
  • 要求更正不準確的資訊
  • 要求刪除您的個人資訊
  • 撤回資料處理的同意

7. Children's Privacy 兒童隱私

Our website is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 12 years of age without parental consent.

我們的網站面向一般受眾，不會在未經父母同意的情況下故意收集 12 歲以下兒童的個人資訊。

8. Updates to This Policy 政策更新

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

我們可能會不時更新本隱私權政策。任何變更都將發布在此頁面上，並附上更新的修訂日期。我們建議您定期查閱本政策。

如果您對本隱私權政策或我們的資料處理方式有任何疑問或疑慮，請聯繫我們：
