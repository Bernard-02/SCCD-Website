MDES WORKS

A collection of advanced research and creative works by graduate students, pushing the boundaries of media design and innovation.

匯集碩士班學生的進階研究與創作，突破媒體設計與創新的界限。

Graduate students at SCCD engage in cutting-edge research and creative practice, exploring emerging technologies and innovative approaches to media design. Through rigorous academic inquiry and hands-on experimentation, they develop original perspectives and contribute to the evolution of the field.

碩士班學生投入前沿研究與創意實踐，探索新興技術與媒體設計的創新方法。透過嚴謹的學術探究與實作實驗，發展原創觀點並為領域發展做出貢獻。

The MDES program emphasizes interdisciplinary collaboration, encouraging students to integrate diverse knowledge domains including technology, design, art, and social sciences. This holistic approach enables the development of innovative solutions for complex contemporary challenges.

碩士課程強調跨領域合作，鼓勵學生整合科技、設計、藝術與社會科學等多元知識領域。這種全面性的方法能夠為當代複雜挑戰開發創新解決方案。

Through mentorship, collaboration, and independent research, graduate students develop the critical thinking, technical skills, and creative vision necessary to become leaders in academia, industry, and the broader creative community.

透過指導、合作與獨立研究，碩士生培養批判性思維、技術能力與創意視野，成為學術界、產業界與創意社群的未來領導者。
