FACULTY

Professors of SCCD are composed of cross-disciplinary experts from academia, design industry and art field. Possess a theoretical and research background in design and art education at domestic and foreign, rich and diverse performance and creative practical experience, and have In-depth understanding of the professional education goals of the department.

實踐大學媒體傳達設計學系專、兼任教師皆為學術界、設計業界與藝術領域專家等跨領域組成，具備國內外設計藝術教育理論研究背景、豐富多樣的展演與創作實務經歷，
