MDES COURSES

Advance your expertise with our Master of Design Studies program, focusing on research and innovation in communications design.

碩士班課程，深入研究媒體傳達設計領域的理論與實務，開拓設計的前衛視野。

1st Year 一年級
Creative Media & Design (I)
數位媒體創作（一）

Design is a complex of different history, society, culture, knowledge, function, aesthetics, problem solving and decision making, through design, human civilization will be gradually escalated and the concept of design will become the scale to evaluate human civilization.

設計是經由不同的歷史、社會和文化層面知識領域的趨勢、作業功能、美學理想、問題解決和決策訂定形成的綜合體，透過這種設計機制以促成提升人類生活文明的品質，因而了解正確的設計概念將成為人類文明比較的重要性指標。
