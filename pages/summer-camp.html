SUMMER
CAMP

An immersive summer experience for aspiring designers, offering hands-on creative workshops and mentorship in a collaborative environment.

實踐大學媒體傳達設計學系 與 嚴長壽主持之 公益平台基金會 共同定期舉辦 暑期高中生設計體驗營，透過短期的沈浸式設計學習體驗，提供高中職學生以與花東偏鄉學生多元的學習課程與經驗，希望啟發學生對設計領域之理解並開發其對於設計之興趣，發掘自己的天賦，讓學生有更多機會與此瞬息萬變之時代脈動接軌。

2025

談一場平凡無奇的戀愛

This immersive summer camp introduces high school students to the fundamentals of design thinking. Through hands-on workshops, collaborative projects, and mentorship from industry professionals, participants will learn how to approach problems creatively and develop innovative solutions. The program covers various design disciplines including graphic design, product design, and spatial design.

這個沉浸式的暑期營隊帶領高中生認識設計思考的基礎。透過實作工作坊、合作專案以及業界專家的指導，學員將學習如何以創意方式解決問題並發展創新方案。課程涵蓋平面設計、產品設計與空間設計等多個設計領域。

2023

我以為我有魔法！

Explore the intersection of art and technology in this intensive workshop. Students will experiment with digital media, interactive installations, and motion graphics. The camp encourages participants to push the boundaries of traditional media and express their unique artistic vision through new technologies.

在這個密集工作坊中探索藝術與科技的交會點。學生將實驗數位媒體、互動裝置與動態圖像。營隊鼓勵學員突破傳統媒體的界限,透過新科技表達獨特的藝術視野。
